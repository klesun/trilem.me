Game Rules
  • There are three players, codenamed KEANU, TRINITY and MORPHEUS, each starts at one of the three tiles in the center of the board
  • Every turn each player chooses one of up to three directions to move his triangular representation or skips his turn by pressing Esc. If there is nowhere to go, turn is skipped automatically
  • Stepping on any tile makes you it's owner: coloring it with your color, making it an obstacle for enemies and increasing your respective resource quantity if it yields one
  • There are three kinds of resources randomly placed on some tiles: Wheat, Oil and Gold. Owned resources form your total score by formula: Wheat x Oil x Gold + Empty. Each player starts with 1 permanent unit of each resource. Here is an example: if you captured 2 tiles of Wheat, 2 tiles of Oil, 2 tiles of Gold and 2 tiles of Empty, your total score is (1 + 2) x (1 + 2) x (1 + 2) + 2 = 29
  • When there are no more turns left, the player with the greatest total score becomes the winner
  • You can step on an enemy owned tile, this will make it belong to you, but if this tile does not hold a resource, you skip ? turn(s) after that as a penalty
  • You can not step on a tile on which another player is currently standing. You can neither step on Dead Space randomly placed in place of some tiles on the board
