Game Rules
  • There are three players, codenamed KEANU, TRINITY and MORPHEUS, each starts at one of the three tiles usually in the center of the board
  • Every turn each player chooses one of up to three directions to move his triangular representation or skips his turn by pressing Esc. If there is nowhere to go, turn is skipped automatically
  • Stepping on any tile makes you it's owner: coloring it with your color, and increasing your respective resource quantity if it yields one
  • There are three kinds of resources randomly placed on some tiles: Wheat, Oil and Gold. Owned resources form your total score by formula: Wheat x Oil x Gold + Empty. Each player starts with 1 permanent unit of each resource.
  • When there are no more turns left, the player with the greatest total score becomes the winner
